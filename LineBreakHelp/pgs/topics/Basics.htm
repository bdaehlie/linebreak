LineBreak Basics

LineBreak is not a complicated program. There are two ways to convert files:

  • Drag files or folders onto LineBreak's icon. You can put LineBreak in the dock for convenience.
  • Select "Convert..." from LineBreak's "File" menu.
There are options you may configure to change the way LineBreak behaves. Make sure you do not tell LineBreak to convert non-text files without first setting protection!

If you don't want LineBreak to change the modification dates on files it converts, turn on "Preserve Modification Dates" under the "LineBreak" menu.
